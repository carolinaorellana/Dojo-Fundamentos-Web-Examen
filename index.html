Welcome to We Collect your IT

We offer a free collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse this equipment by ensuring any sensitive or personal information that was on these machine is either physically removed or destroyec before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benefit the environment by avoiding the waste and disposal of this equipment but shall also help others, as we donate and proportion of all the equipment we collect.
